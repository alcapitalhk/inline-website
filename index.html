Company Overview

Who we are

With a current strategic focus on Australia, we serve as a reliable bridge between trusted Chinese manufacturers and clients in the real estate, technology and furniture industries — delivering quality, value and dependable supply at scale.

We are more than a trading desk. We are a long-term sourcing partner built on direct factory relationships, rigorous vetting and end-to-end logistics that get the right materials to the right project, on time.
